Woman2WomanEmpowerment

Woman2WomanEmpowerment is the leading heart and mind charity for underprivileged women. We are committed to giving every underprivileged woman and young girl a voice. We work in the space deemed as poor, indigent, unsuitable and uncultured, helping women and young girls tackle the barriers they face and supporting them to create empowering lives for themselves, their families and the community.

We recognize that the underprivileged women and girls are disproportionately affected by poverty and discriminations and suffer abuse mentally and violations in the realization of their rights, entitlements and access to central resources. Our experience has shown us that when equipped with the proper resources, women and young girls have the power to transform their own lives and help their entire family, hence readdressing the cause of poverty and social injustice in the community.

What we do

We deliver skill development training programs for women and young girls in our society • We work with adolescent girls to protect their health and rights by providing them access to sexual and reproductive health counselling, information and services; comprehensive sexuality education • We co-operate and assist in suitable joint venture with the Governmental Organizations and agencies seeking the goal of redressing poverty and improving health status of women • We organize women empowerment, capacity and skills development programs aimed at enabling them to become economically and socially independent • We provide immediate and emergency help in crisis

Our Mission

Our mission is embedded in our mandate which is to empower underprivileged women and young girls through relevant education, skills development, innovative health care and focused livelihood programs.
